About XL family

  • by Marc van Gent

    CEO

"We believe in the value of co-creation - our clients are an extension of our family" - Marc van Gent, CEO XL family

XL family is an innovation company with a digital twist. We activate companies for innovation, design & build cool solutions and co-create new business with them. This way we guide empower our clients in their entrepreneurial endeavors, elevating ideas from concept to market.

What makes us different is our team(=our family). We are a blend of strategic thinkers and creative minds, passionate about innovation, new technology and beautiful designs.

What drives us forward is raising ideas that make a difference and deliver real value. The ideas that go beyond a product or a service. The game-changers. The brave, risky, daring ideas that get people talking and light-up entrepreneurial spirits. Including our entrepreneurial spirits.

Did we mention we invest in our own adventures? We do! As entrepreneurs we are actively scouting for new projects and opportunities to invest in promising startups, extraordinary in their markets.

To wrap this up in one package, we combine our knowledge and experience from working with corporates and learning with startups to deliver innovation and build tomorrow.
